Our Story

Prasuna & Prasuna have been best friends for close to a decade. We do everything together, so when the time came, it only made sense that we open a kitchen together, too!

We immigrated to America from South India in the 1990s. So far from home, we realized just how much comfort and love a simple home-cooked meal can give. We are so excited and honored to be able to give this gift to each and every one of our customers. We serve the freshest, most authentic, and most flavorful dishes, desserts, and snacks we possibly can-- come in and have a taste!
